Mara Voss
Photographer & Visual Artist

Based between Berlin and Cairo, I work across portrait, landscape, editorial, and documentary photography. My work explores the quiet tension between stillness and motion — the fraction of a second where truth is visible before the world resumes.

Published in Vogue, Aperture, and Le Monde. Awards include the World Press Photo Award and Prix de la Photographie Paris. Available for editorial commissions, commercial campaigns, and private sessions worldwide.
